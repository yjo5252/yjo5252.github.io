YEEUN JO😎

Graduate Research Assistant

Yeeun Jo is a first-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign.

She is advised by Professor Adam Bates and Professor Camille Cobb.

Her research interests span human-computer interaction, design, and security/privacy.

In the Secure & Transparent Systems Laboratory , she is currently investigating issues of privacy and safety in location-based mobile apps.

Before joining Illinois, Yeeun double majored in Computer Science & Engineering and Digital Humanities at Ewha Womans University, Seoul, South Korea.
