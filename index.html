YEEUN JO

Yeeun Jo is a researcher in human computer interaction and a PhD graduate research assistant in Computer Science at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign.

She is advised by Professor Camille Cobb and Professor Adam Bates.

Her research interests span human-computer interaction, design, and security/privacy.

In the Secure & Transparent Systems Lab , she investigates issues of security in location-based mobile apps.

Before joining Illinois, Yeeun double majored in Computer Science & Engineering and Digital Humanities at Ewha Womans University, Seoul, South Korea.

Contact viayeeunjo2@illinois.edu.
