Researcher & Designer

Ye Eun Jo is a senior student double majored in Computer Science & Engineering and Digital Humanities at Ewha Womans University, Seoul, South Korea.

Her work, based on a broad spectrum of computing technologies and design, investigates fundamental questions on how people interact with the world. She is an intern working at Multicore Computing Research Lab at Seoul National University working on a Language Model and Natural Language Processing Project.

Her research interests are in the areas of Natrual Language Processing (NLP), Human-AI Interaction, and Graphic Design. She also entertained by creating new software programs and designing UI/UX services.
