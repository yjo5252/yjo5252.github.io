Yeeun Jo

I'm a researcher in human-computer interaction, currently a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign working with Professor Camille Cobb and Professor Adam Bates. My research focuses on human-computer interaction. My work aims to portray patterns in data and build solutions to address users’ needs in areas of user experiences, security, and privacy.

I graduated from Ewha Womans University with a B.S. in Computer Science & Engineering and a B.A. in Digital Humanities at Seoul, South Korea. During undergrad years, I did a research building doument computational analysis software, 3D printed drone, and analysis on technical factors in interactive media arts.
